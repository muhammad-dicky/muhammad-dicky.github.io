Here is my recent exploration design for Event Web Concept. I think that most of you know how difficult it can be to find interesting concerts. I decided to create an app to make it as enjoyable as possible. Therefore, here is app for discover the most interesting music events and get personalised recommendations. It also allows you to check the event details, buy a ticket and keep it on the app. With this app you will be able to keep up to date with all interesting music events.
